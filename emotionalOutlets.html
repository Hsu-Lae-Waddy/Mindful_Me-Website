Emotional Outlets

Discovering healthy ways to process feelings and cultivate inner peace

Pathways to Peace of mind

Explore different categories of emotional outlets and find what resonates with you

Physical exercise

Exercise and other physical activity can lessen anxiety and help mood and other health problems get better.

Journaling

Regularly recording thoughts, emotions, and experiences to boost self-awareness, reduce stress, and manage anxiety or depression

Music Therapy

Listen to smoothing music

Mindfulness

Practice being present and aware of your thoughts.

Creative Art

Creative arts—including painting, music, dance, writing, and, crafts—enhance mental health by reducing stress (cortisol), increasing "feel-good" hormones (endorphins), and fostering a meditative "flow" state.
