Journal Writing ✍️

Writing down your thoughts can help you understand your feelings and release stress.

Take a quiet moment and write in your notebook.

1️⃣: How do I feel right now?

Write a few words about your emotions.

2️⃣: What happened today?

Describe an important moment.

3️⃣: What am I grateful for?

Write at least one positive thing.

4️⃣: What do I need right now?

Rest, support, motivation, or kindness.

Take your time. There are no right or wrong answers. ✨
