Journal Writing ✍️

Writing down your thoughts can help you understand your feelings and release stress.

Take a quiet moment and write in your notebook.

1️⃣

Write a few words about your emotions.

2️⃣

Describe an important moment.

3️⃣: What am I grateful for?

Write at least one positive thing.

4️⃣: What do I need right now?

Rest, support, motivation, or kindness.

If you feel stressed

- What is causing my stress today?

- What would help me feel calmer?

If you feel sad

- What is making me feel sad?

- What can comfort me right now?

If you feel happy

- What made me feel happy today?

- How can I keep this feeling?

5-Minute Journal Structure

5 Minute Reflection

Morning

  • What am I grateful for?
  • What is one goal for today?

Evening

  • What went well today?
  • What did I learn?
Take your time. There are no right or wrong answers. ✨
