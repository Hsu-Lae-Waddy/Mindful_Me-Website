Muscle Relaxation

When overwhelmed, the body holds tension.

Step: 1 Tighten your fists for 5 seconds → release.
Step: 2 shoulders tightly → release.
Step: 3 Clench jaw → release.
Step: 4 Press toes downward → release.

×

Cognitive Reset Questions

After calming your body, ask:

  • What exactly am I afraid will happen?
  • Is this a current threat or a future possibility?
  • What is one small action I can take now?

Overwhelm often comes from vagueness. Precision reduces anxiety.
